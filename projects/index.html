Optimizing Home Layout with CNNs

Machine Learning Research Paper

Small apartments and rooms are common in Singapore due to its limited land. To optimize the home layout, we trained a machine learning model with CNNs to predict the ideal location to position furniture for a given 2D floor plan. Our model allows users to visualize potential optimized layout of their homes before they commit to furnishing.
